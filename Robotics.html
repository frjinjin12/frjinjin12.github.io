Robotics

Robotics has played a major role in my life. It is one of my favorite hobbies, and I see it as a future career path.

I got in to robotics at the young age of 5. Before that, I loved building advanced-Lego sets and watching videos about technology. I have helped create and be a part of our schools robotics club. We have made many projects as a team, building a functioning bridge, greenhouse and we have also participated in a underwater robotics camp. However, due to our small school size, we constantly lacked members and resources leading us to miss many opportunites and competition.

It started as simple robotics kits where I learned about different mechanics and basics of engineering. When I was in year 3, I started to learn coding from an online website. My mom suggested that I should try to connect this skill with my robotics skill to create something even more advanced. With that advice, I was introduced to a whole new world. One of the first projects I built was a Rubiks cube solver.

Now, robotics is how I contribute to the world. I hope with my skills and determiantion, I can develop technology to support the world.
